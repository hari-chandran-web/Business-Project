## Interview Walkthrough

Start with the business problem:

"The company was growing revenue, but leadership did not know whether that growth was profitable. I analyzed orders, products, customers, discounts, and delivery performance to find where margin was leaking and which customer segments were worth retaining."

Then explain your method:

"I created core KPIs, segmented performance by country, region, customer segment, product category, and acquisition channel, and used SQL for reporting queries. I used Python to generate and validate the dataset and create monthly KPI tables for dashboarding."

Then explain impact:

"The analysis supports decisions around discount strategy, product prioritization, logistics improvement, and churn-risk win-back campaigns."

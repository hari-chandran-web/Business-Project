# Dashboard Specification

## Page 1: Executive Overview

Audience: Head of Growth, Country Manager, Finance Manager.

Visuals:

- KPI cards: revenue, gross profit, gross margin %, orders, repeat customer rate, SLA breach rate
- Monthly revenue and gross margin line chart
- Country and region revenue/profit map or bar chart
- Top 5 profit drivers and top 5 margin leakages

Decision supported:

- Is growth profitable?
- Which regions deserve more investment?
- Where is margin leaking?

## Page 2: Profitability Diagnostics

Visuals:

- Category/product matrix: revenue vs gross margin %
- Discount band profitability bar chart
- Delivery cost as % of revenue by country/region
- Product ranking table with conditional formatting

Decision supported:

- Which products should be promoted, repriced, bundled, or deprioritized?
- Which discount bands destroy profit?

## Page 3: Customer Retention

Visuals:

- Repeat rate by acquisition channel
- Churn-risk customers by country and segment
- Segment profitability table
- Customer cohort heatmap if using BI tool supports it

Decision supported:

- Which channels attract valuable repeat customers?
- Which customer segments need win-back campaigns?

## Page 4: Delivery Performance

Visuals:

- SLA breach rate by country, city, and segment
- Delivery cost trend over time
- Late order impact on repeat rate

Decision supported:

- Which regions need logistics improvement?
- Is late delivery linked to lower retention?

## Page 5: Recommendations

Recommended actions:

1. Reduce blanket discounts above 20% unless attached to high-repeat segments.
2. Focus growth spending on channels with high repeat rate and positive gross margin.
3. Review products with high revenue but low gross profit for pricing, shipping, or supplier renegotiation.
4. Create a 90-day churn-risk win-back campaign segmented by country and customer type.
5. Track delivery SLA as an operating KPI, not just a logistics metric.

## Design Notes

- Use filters for country, region, segment, category, month, and acquisition channel.
- Keep executive KPIs at the top.
- Use green only for profitable performance and red only for risk/leakage.
- Include a short recommendation panel on every page.
